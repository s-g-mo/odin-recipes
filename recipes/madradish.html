Mad Radish Surprise

Return to the recipe directory

Description

Let your expectations of what a radish is supposed to be, be challenged by this amazing dish. You'll never look at radishes the same again!

Ingredients

  • A bundle of filthy radishes
  • A finely distilled spirit (at least 40% a.b.v.)
  • Goat hair

Steps

  1. Dig up a bundle of filthsome radishes (best if fresh from the garden)
  2. Soak in lukewarm water for 72 hours
  3. After soaking, empty water from pot, pour in 1 L of spirit, and continue to boil until the spirit has totally evaporated
  4. Slice into thin circular sections, sprinkle with goat hair, and serve immediately
